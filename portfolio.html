Maps

Figure 3.1:Saskatoon Population Density Map - This map was created in 2021 and it outlines Saskatoon’s most populated metropolitan areas. The white areas indicate that the population is sparse and as the population per area gets larger/denser the color shifts from a light pink to dark pink and then finally to purple. The purple indicates the most populated areas
