Our Background

JABRS was created over a year ago with the intention of creating an easy interface in which our customers would feel comfortable working with us. Each of the four founders has an interesting background that positively influences not only company but also our clients to look at their problems in their own way and to come up with solutions that fit their needs.

Key Features of our Company

JABRS Ltd is a consulting company that has your best interests at heart! We strive for perfection with every client we have. The name JABRS comes from a colaboratative effort between the five members of the group that started the company.

Responsive

We are always available through our email to assess any questions or changes you would like to make to your project. We pride ourselves on our ability to create unbelievable relationships with our clients.

Passion

We started JABRS because we loved the work before and we thought this was an amazing opportunity to turn our passion into our full time job. We want to make Geographical Analysis easy to understand as well as accessible to everyone.

Support
